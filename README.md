# Car Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project

 Interactive Car Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze vehicle pricing, mileage trends, fuel efficiency, and transmission types through data-driven insights.

---

## Executive Summary

I developed a Car Sales Power BI dashboard to provide an interactive overview of vehicle characteristics and sales-related metrics. The dashboard analyzes important attributes such as **price, mileage, fuel efficiency (MPG), and tax**, enabling users to better understand vehicle specifications and compare different car categories.

The dashboard highlights key performance indicators including an **average vehicle price of around $11K**, **average mileage near 23K miles**, and **fuel efficiency around 59–63 MPG**. It also compares **transmission types (Manual, Automatic, Semi-Auto)** and different **fuel types such as Hybrid, Electric, Diesel, and Petrol** to help users explore vehicle trends more effectively.

---

## Business Problem

Automotive companies and dealerships often work with large datasets containing vehicle specifications such as **price, mileage, fuel efficiency, transmission type, and fuel type**. Without proper data visualization, it becomes difficult to identify patterns and trends in vehicle characteristics.

Decision makers need an analytical dashboard that answers key questions such as:

- What is the average price and mileage of vehicles?
- Which fuel types dominate the market?
- What transmission types are most common?
- How do vehicle specifications change across years?

Without a centralized analytical dashboard, analyzing these patterns becomes difficult and time-consuming.

## KPI Summary

- **Average Car Price:** $11K  
- **Average Mileage:** ~23K miles

- **Transmission Types:** Manual | Automatic | Semi-Auto  
- **Fuel Types:** Hybrid | Electric | Diesel | Petrol | Other  

---

## Key Insights

- **Stable vehicle pricing:** The average vehicle price across the dataset is approximately $11K.

- **Moderate mileage levels:** Most vehicles fall around 22K–23K miles, indicating relatively low usage.

- **Strong fuel efficiency:** Vehicles in the dataset show fuel efficiency around 60 MPG, suggesting a high presence of efficient or hybrid cars.

- **Transmission distribution:** Manual transmission vehicles dominate, followed by automatic and semi-automatic.

- **Fuel type trends:** Hybrid vehicles show the highest average prices, followed by electric and diesel vehicles.

---

## Business Recommendations

- **Promote fuel-efficient vehicles:** Hybrid and electric vehicles demonstrate strong value and should be prioritized in marketing strategies.

- **Analyze transmission demand:** Although manual transmissions dominate, the market trend may shift toward automatic vehicles.

- **Highlight low mileage vehicles:** Cars with lower mileage often command higher value and should be emphasized in listings.

- **Leverage analytics dashboards:** Automotive businesses can use similar dashboards to better understand vehicle pricing trends and optimize sales strategies.
